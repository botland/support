# AI chat workspace

Every OwnEdge appliance includes an **AI chat workspace** for day-to-day use by staff — private chat with models that run on the appliance.

## What it is for

- Team assistants (writing, review, analysis)
- Internal knowledge conversations
- Prototyping prompts against models you deployed in the console

## How it relates to the console

- The **console** deploys and sizes **models**
- The **chat workspace** is where people **talk to** those models
- Capacity and concurrency depend on hardware tier and active deployments

## Privacy

Chats stay on the customer network with the models you host. This is not a public multi-tenant chat service.
