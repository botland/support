# Document knowledge (private Q&A)

OwnEdge appliances support **document knowledge**: answers grounded in the customer’s own files, without uploading corpora to a public AI provider.

## Typical uses

- Internal knowledge assistant
- Search and Q&A over contracts, reports, and policies
- Find clauses and facts while keeping content on-premises

## How staff experience it

1. Documents are made available to the appliance (local storage, mounts, or approved connectors)
2. Users ask questions in the chat workspace or knowledge tools
3. Answers cite or draw from the private corpus when configured

## Relation to models

Document Q&A uses models you deploy (and capacity you sized) on the appliance. Larger tiers support more concurrent users and larger corpora in practice.
