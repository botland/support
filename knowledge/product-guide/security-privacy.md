# Security and privacy

## Ownership

Customers own the hardware, the models they place on it, and the data they process.

## Network

- Day-to-day inference and chat stay on the **local network**
- No requirement to keep a permanent cloud control plane for routine operations
- Optional outbound support diagnostics only when an admin chooses **Send diagnostic report**

## Accounts third parties

- **Hugging Face**: required when pulling gated or Hub-hosted models; customers create their own Hugging Face account and tokens
- OwnEdge does not replace Hugging Face authentication for Hub downloads

## Secrets

Diagnostic bundles are redacted. Admins should not paste secrets into support notes or into this product guide chat.
