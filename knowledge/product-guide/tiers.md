# Appliance tiers

Public names: **Edge**, **Studio**, **Forge**. Model sizes below are typical fit — not hard limits.

## Edge

- Desk-side / small office form factor
- About **1–5 concurrent users**
- Typical models in the **≈7B–13B** class
- Best for: solo use, labs, small businesses
- Quiet, ready in minutes, room to grow later

## Studio

- Team capacity
- About **10–30 concurrent users**
- Typical models up to **≈70B** class
- Best for: teams, agencies, scale-ups
- Shared production use

## Forge

- Department / enterprise scale
- Large and multi-model workloads; multi-unit ready
- Best for: enterprise and research
- Maximum headroom; expand into a private fleet

## Platform differences (functional)

- **Multi-node cluster**: available on all tiers
- **Cluster coordinator (head)**: Studio and Forge (not Edge as a dedicated multi-unit head role in the product story)
- **Group / fleet management**: Forge-oriented

Exact hardware defaults (RAM, VRAM, disk, network) are chosen in the B2B configurator on the OwnEdge site.
