# OwnEdge appliances — overview

OwnEdge sells **private AI appliances**: complete hardware + software you own and run on your network. Day-to-day use stays local. Models and data remain under your custody.

## What you get

- **Hardware** you purchase once (Edge, Studio, or Forge capacity classes)
- **Console** — web management UI (Overview, Models, Orchestration, Nodes, Storage, System, Support, Configuration)
- **AI chat workspace** — team chat with models running on the appliance
- **Document knowledge** — private Q&A over your own files
- **Connectors** — tools that reach approved files, local network services, or the web when policy allows
- **Model training & customization** — improve models on hardware you own
- **Optional care** — Managed Care and SecureVault Backup (monthly, per appliance)

## How capacity is organized (short)

- **Standalone** — one appliance is the whole system  
- **Distributed** — multi-unit cluster with a **coordinator (head)** and **workers**  
- Inside distributed (and related serving choices), the console also chooses **Federated inference** vs **Clustered inference** (see Orchestration knowledge). Do not oversimplify multi-node to only “distributed.”

## Who uses it

Existing staff can operate the appliance for routine work: deploy models, watch health, grow capacity, and use chat and document tools on the local network.

## Privacy posture

Inference and document work run on your premises. Optional diagnostic sharing with OwnEdge support is **opt-in** from the console Support section (sanitized reports only).
